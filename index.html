NETWORK TRANSACTION TERMS · TAX LIABILITIES · SLASHING PROTOCOL

XeroCoins (XC) are non-custodial digital credits issued by the XeroCloud Mesh protocol. All earnings are accrued in XC and converted to fiat at the fixed rate of 1 XC = $2.00 USD at time of payout initiation. This rate is subject to periodic review and may fluctuate for future payout cycles; balances already credited remain protected under the locked conversion index.

TAX LIABILITY: Operators are solely responsible for declaring computational income to their regional tax authorities. XeroCloud Mesh does not withhold, remit, or advise on tax obligations. Earnings may constitute self-employment income, digital asset income, or passive income depending on your jurisdiction. Consult a qualified tax professional.

SLASHING PROTOCOL: Any abrupt termination of an active compute stream — including system shutdowns, network disconnection, or manual process throttling — constitutes a protocol violation. The XeroCloud penalty engine will automatically slash (void) all accrued rewards for the interrupted session. Repeat violations may result in reduced yield rates or temporary node suspension.

PAYOUT FINALITY: Once a payout transaction is authorized, it is irreversible. Ensure your wallet address or payment endpoint is correct before initiating. XeroCloud Mesh accepts no liability for funds sent to incorrect destinations. Disputed transactions must be filed within 7 calendar days of issuance.

DECENTRALIZED LEDGER: All transaction records are immutably recorded on the XeroCloud distributed ledger. This constitutes your official earnings record. Operators may export this data for personal accounting purposes at any time via the Developer Core export API.
